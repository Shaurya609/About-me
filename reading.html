Reading

Novels


Harry Potter

This story follows the life of a 11 year boy Harry Potter in a normal world. Little does he know that he is a wizard who survived the killing curse of the most infamous Dark Lord Voldemort. Harry life changes when a bear like man Hagrid informs him that he is wizard enrolled for the most famous wizarding school Hogwarts School Of Witchcraft And Wizardary.Will Harry defeat the Dark Lord and end his reign of terror?

Percy Jackson

Percy Jackson is a problematic kid with ADHD and dyslexia, he got kicked out of six schools in six years and currently studies in Yale Academy. His normal life changes when he is attacked by him mathematics teacher as a demon lady. Now he has to go to a special camp called "Camp Half Blood" where he gets to know that he is accused of stealing the most destructive weapon, Zeus' lightning bolt. Little does he know he is half human and half Greek god.

Heroes Of Olympus

Set after the finish of the war against Titans in Percy Jackson series. This story introduces roman gods. Greek and Romans have had a bad past and hold grudges, Percy Jackson is missing, mother of all Titans Gaia is on the brink of waking up and consuming the earth, seven demigods Greek and Roman have to stop Gaia. This book is full of action, adventure and humor. Can the Greeks and Romans unite? or will they hold on to the ancient grudges and fight each other while Gaia awakes and consumes everything?

Magnus Chase

Magnus Chase is based on Norse mythology, where warriors after there death go to Valhalla (Viking heaven) to train till Ragnarok (Viking doomsday) and to serve Odin in the mighty battle. The main character (Magnus Chase) dies in the beginning of the story and thus goes to Valhalla. Now he must train endlessly to prepare for Ragnarok. Loki, the god of mischief is on the loose can Magnus stop him?



Kane Chronicles

Sadie and Carter Kane are siblings who separated when their mother died; Sadie with her grandparents and Carter with his father. Their life went into a turmoil when they get to know that their parents are magicians from the House Of Magic and all the Egyptian gods are alive and kicking. Sadie and Carter should become magicians themselves to prevent the rise of Set, the god of Chaos and Apophis, chaos itself.

Trial Of Apollo

This story follows after the Heroes Of Olympus series as the God Apollo is punished by Zeus by putting him in a mortal shell. He is forced to serve a twelve year old girl and obey her every order. Taking advantage of Apollo's weakness his enemies seize the control of every oracle. Apollo must retake every oracle in his mortal form to hope forgiveness from Zeus.

Sherlock Holmes

Sherlock Holmes is an introvert with god like deduction abilities. He acts as a consulting detective to the people in the need of such.The story of his adventures solving inhumanly difficult cases is told by his companion and roommate, Doctor Watson. The book compromises of many short stories and 4-5 long stories. Sherlock Holmes also meets a match to his abilities in Doctor Moriarty.

Divergent

Again set in the dystopian future of America, Divergent is a story about people split into factions according to their aptitude test results. These factions are:
Abnegation(selfless)
Erudite(intellectual)
Amity(peaceful)
Candor(honest)
Dauntless(brave)
Beatrice's results are inconclusive and indicate three factions- Abnegation, Erudite, Dauntless. This marks her as a "divergent" and in this world divergent are hunted like dogs....



The Fault in Our Stars

Hazel has lung cancer and is pretty much depressed with no longer the will to live. She goes to the support group in church which she finds boring and depressing. She loves a novel was An Imperial Affliction and she wants to meets its author. The story sets afoot when a boy in the support group she likes, asks her out. Suddenly she has the will to live and something to look forward to. They both set afoot an adventure to find An Imperial Affliction's author.

Gone

This story is set in Pedrido Beach also know as "Fallout Alley" due to an asteroid collision with the nuclear plant many years ago. It was declared "safe" by the authorities and Sam and his parents live here. The story starts when anyone aged 15 and above disappears, this leaves the young ones in charge which seeming fun at the start is indeed a dire situation. Now the children have too take care for themselves and toddlers. Suddenly its a world without adults and the oldest kids have to step up and take charge. Some kids like Sam develop super powers, what is the reason? Will they survive? You will only know by reading.

The Chronicles Of Narnia

This novel series follows the adventures of four siblings, two brothers and two sisters through a different world Narnia. In this world, animals can talk and can fight too. Narnia is ruled over by evil and these four siblings help the locals overcome evil and restore justice in Narnia.



Comics



Tintin

Tintin is a reporter for the French newspaper le Petit Vengtime who roams the world with his faithful companion dog Snowy. The story revolves around these two as they visit many places in the world unravelling controversies, demasking the corrupt and bringing down underground mafias.

Asterix and Obelix

Asterix and Obelix are Gauls who live in a indomitable village of Gauls in an era where Cesar has conquered every Gaul village. This comic follows the adventure of Asterix and Obelix throughout Rome and beyond. Caesar tries his very best to take over their village but they are in possession of a secret potion which gives them superhuman strength.

The Walking Dead

Rick Grimes is a policeman who after suffering a grievous injury, goes into coma. He wakes up in a world full of zombies, where it is survival of the fittest. This comic series progresses as Rick tries to find his wife and son, and his struggle to survive in a world where its either kill or get killed. Will Rick survive the environment? Will there be any rescue? Is there any cure? Is anybody behind this horrible incident? This all you can only know by reading.



Manga



One Piece

The Pirate King Gold D. Roger is the only person who has dominated all of the seas. After doing so, he leaves all his wealth in one place and before dying at the hand of marine he says "Want all my wealth? I have left it at one place!". This wealth is referred to as "One Piece". 11 year Monkey D. Luffy wants to be the next pirate king. The story follows his adventures through the Grand Line as he gathers a crew and work his way towards the One Piece.

Dragon Ball

Son Goku is a pretty strong kid. He lives alone when because his grandfather was killed in an accident. The only memory of his long lost grandfather Goku has a golden ball with four stars on it. His life takes a turn when a girl named Bulma enters his life telling him that the ball is indeed a Dragon Ball and there are seven such balls scattered across the Earth, the person who collects all of them will be granted his/her choice by the dragon Shenlon. This story follows the life of Goku as he grows up and strives to be the strongest fighter in the universe.

Death Note

Yagami Raito is an ace student bored to death by his life, but this all changes when he obtains a notebook in which the person whose name is written, dies. This notebook is a Death Note. Yagami aims to clean the world of its crime by using this Death Note. Will he succeed? Or will he turn into what he is trying to get rid of?

Naruto

Naruto is a very naughty kid who lives in the village of Leaf,a village full of ninjas. Naruto wants to become the fourth chief of the village but he has to work very hard to achieve that goal. That's not all long time ago a nine tail beast had attacked the village, it was defeated by sealing the beast in Naruto as a baby.Naruto has to take control of the beast within him. The story follows the life of Naruto as he works towards his goal.



Bleach

Since he was a kid, Kurosaki Ichigo had the ability to see ghosts and help them. He has a father and two little sisters, his mother died when he was a kid. His father is a doctor in a sloppy clinic he owns. His normal life takes a wild turn of events when out of the blue he meets Kuchiki Rukia who claims to be a Shinigami (death god). Ichigo is forced to take on the Shinigami job to protect his family and friends. This story progresses as Ichigo grows stronger and defeats greater evil to maintain peace in both human world and the Soul Society.

Fairy Tail

Fairy Tail is a story about mages which work for guilds, it revolves around the main character Natsu Dragneel, "the dragon son" who is a fire mage, he can craft and create fire at his will. There are many "dark" guilds which do not follow the rules of the government. The story revolves around clashes between "good" and "evil" guilds.

Assassination Classroom

Ever thought "I wish I could just kill that teacher"?. Well students of class 3-E have been tasked with exactly that. They have to kill their teacher who destroyed the moon because in one year he has threatened to destroy Earth as well. Pretty easy no? Well not for them as the teacher has a speed of Mach 20 (20 times speed of sound). Due to this insane speed and tactics the students named him Korosensie (Koro-Un-killable, Sensie-Teacher in Japanese) And has regenerative properties. Can these kids kill their teacher? Can the professional assassins? How come this teacher has such abilities? You will only know when you read.

Full Metal Alchemist

Alchemy is a exact science, what is created will be destroyed. In the world of national alchemists there are few rules: Not to convert stone to gold, Not to do human transmutation (creating of life and experiments with humans) and To always follow orders (i.e. to be the army dog). Edward and Alphonse Elric are the youngest alchemist but they committed a crime when they tried to revive their dead mother, thus making Edward loose his right arm and left leg replaced by prosthetic steel limbs (thus giving him the name "Full Metal Alchemist") and Alphonse loosing his body. Alphonse was saved when Edward transmuted his soul into a suit of armor. This story follow the adventures of the Elric brothers as they both try to restore their bodies uncovering the secrets of the army along the way.



Hunter x Hunter

Hunters are skilled people who hunt for various rare beasts and objects, they also help the government maintain order and are helpful to the common people. Gon's father Jin Freaks is a very well known and strong hunter, but Gon has never met him since his was born. This story sets out as Gon undertakes an adventure to find his father by starting of as becoming a hunter himself. Would Gon survive the hardships of being a hunter? Will he find his father? Will he defeat evil? Those things can only be known by reading this manga series.

One Punch Man

Saitama is too strong, he ends every fight with one punch and thus has lost the thrill of a fight. He joins the hero association and is ranked C class. The story progresses as more heroes flock around him, eventually becoming his apprentices. The monsters make their own association and launch a whole scale attack on the humans with the plan to crush the hero association. Will Saitama be able to save humanity? It is still a ongoing manga so even I don't know :p.

Tokyo Ghoul

Ghouls are creatures that eat humans and look like them. The city of Tokyo has a rising amount of ghoul population in it, to counter this the government has formed an association called CCG(Commission of Counter Ghoul).The story revolves around the main character Ken Kaneki as being a normal human, he undergoes surgery after a critical accident that transforms him to a half ghoul. Shunned by both ghouls and humans he wants to achieve a world where humans and ghouls come to mutual existence.

Dragon Ball super

Set after Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Super begins with Goku working as a farmer (strange right?) and trying to live a normal life. This all is changed when the God of destruction (Beerus) pays a visit to Earth looking for the strongest fighter (which is Goku. Duh). But Beerus defeats Goku in super saiyan 3 form without as even the need to lift two fimgers. Now Goku has to train hard and power up to get to his level. The Universe matches are approaching, will Goku be able to make it? This series is another classic by Akira Toriyama.



Dr. Slump

Dr. Slump is a gag manga written by the world renown mangaka Akira Toriyama (creator of Dragon Ball Z for you dumb people). It follows the story of Arale a robot created by Dr. Slump. Arale is a robot that Sengoku meets during his travels. This story is creation of Arale and her mischiefs. It is a very hilarious manga and a should read for those who love Dragon Ball. For Akira Toriyama the creator of children's dreams to draw this story is awesome.

Berserk

Berserk is a story about a mercenary named Gats who has a sword as long as his body. The story progresses as he is marked as a "sacrifice" by the leader of his mercenary group who he considered as his best friend and mentor. The leader marks everyone in the group as sacrifice so as to possess inhumane powers. Gats escaped the sacrifice and is haunted by demons and monsters and his lover is also marked by same symbol
